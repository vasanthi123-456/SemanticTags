Introduction

MS Dhoni is an iconic Indian cricketer known as "Captain Cool" for his calm leadership and strategic brilliance, guiding India to victory in the 2007 T20 World Cup, 2011 World Cup, and 2013 Champions Trophy. A renowned wicket-keeper and a brilliant lower-order batsman, he is celebrated for his finishing skills, unique "helicopter shot," and inspiring journey from a small town to global sports stardom. His influential career, which includes leading the Chennai Super Kings to multiple IPL titles, has left an indelible mark on cricket, making him a symbol of sportsmanship and perseverance.
